    Frequently Asked Questions

    We understand that you may have many questions about our services. Therefore, we have compiled a list of frequently asked questions along with their answers. We hope this information will help you better understand what we offer and how we can serve you better.

    • How to order and come to the Dealer?

      Buyers are expected to schedule an appointment in advance via WhatsApp. We will inform you on H-3 or H-2 whether the car is still available.

    • What if want to book the car and until the day before, the buyer will pay in full?

      Buyers must pay a minimum down payment of IDR 50,000,000 or 3200 USD. until the D day so that his dream car can be protected from other buyers.

    • Does payment accept credit?

      Yes, but it would be better if the buyer schedules a meeting and meets at the showroom, so that the discussion about car financing can be done more details and relaxedly.

    • Can the car be shipped abroad or exported?

      Yes, we offer international shipping and export services for our cars. For payments we only accept from Bank Central Asia and Seabank, if other than that, it must be discussed on Whatsapp and I suggest you just come to our showroom so us can discuss it better.

    • Can anyone sell cars in our showroom online??

      Yes and are required to have a fix and potential buyer, but we can only give commissions above 10,000,000 IDR and below 50,000,000 IDR per unit and for more clarity and details regarding someone as an agent, you can contact WhatsApp for further explanation.
